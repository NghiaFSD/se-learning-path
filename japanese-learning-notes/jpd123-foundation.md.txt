## 📝 1. Major Verb Conjugations (Thể Biến Đổi Động Từ)

### 🔹 Thể て (Te-form) - Lesson 14
*(Refer to `te-form-conjugation.md` for deep-dive rules)*
- **Usage:** Used for requests (`～てください`), continuous states (`～ています`), permissions (`～てもいいです`), and prohibitions (`～てはいけません`).

### 🔹 Thể ない (Nai-form / Negative Plain) - Lesson 17
- **Group 1:** Change `~i` sound to `~a` sound + `nai` (e.g., *kaimasu* $\rightarrow$ *kawanai*).
- **Group 2:** Drop `~masu` + `nai` (e.g., *tabemasu* $\rightarrow$ *tabenai*).
- **Group 3:** *shimasu* $\rightarrow$ *shinai* | *kimasu* $\rightarrow$ *konai*.
- **Grammar Patterns:**
  - `V-ないで ください` (Please don't do...).
  - `V-なければ なりません` (Must do / Obligation).
  - `V-なくても いいです` (No need to do...).

### 🔹 Thể 辞書形 (Dictionary Form / Present Plain) - Lesson 18
- **Group 1:** Change `~i` sound to `~u` sound (e.g., *ikimasu* $\rightarrow$ *iku*).
- **Group 2:** Drop `~masu` + `ru` (e.g., *tabemasu* $\rightarrow$ *taberu*).
- **Group 3:** *shimasu* $\rightarrow$ *suru* | *kimasu* $\rightarrow$ *kuru*.
- **Grammar Patterns:**
  - `V-辞書形 ことができます` (Can do / Ability).
  - `趣味/夢は V-辞書形 ことです` (My hobby/dream is...).
  - `V-辞書形 / Nの / Time まえに、～` (Before doing something...).

### 🔹 Thể た (Ta-form / Past Plain) - Lesson 19
- **Conjugation Rule:** Exactly identical to the **て-form**, but replace `て/で` with `た/だ`.
- **Grammar Patterns:**
  - `V-た ことがあります` (Have experienced doing something before).
  - `V-た り、V-た り します` (Listing actions randomly).

## 📝 2. Advanced Sentence Patterns

### 🔹 Thể Thông Thường (Plain Form / Futsuukei) - Lesson 20
- The foundational system used with friends, family, and as sub-clauses in advanced grammar.

### 🔹 Clauses & Modifications (Lesson 21 - 23)
- **Opinion:** `[Plain Form] と 思います` (I think that...).
- **Quotation:** `[Sentence] と 言いました` (He/She said that...).
- **Noun Modification:** Using a short-form verb phrase to modify a noun (e.g., *Watashi ga tsukutta keeki* - The cake that I made).
- **Conditionals (とき & と):**
  - `V-辞書形 / V-た とき、～` (When doing...).
  - `V-辞書形 と、～` (If/Whenever this happens, the consequence naturally follows).

### 🔹 Giving & Receiving Actions (Lesson 24)
- `V-て あげます` (Doing a favor for someone).
- `V-て もらいます` (Receiving a favor from someone).
- `V-て くれます` (Someone doing a favor for me/my family).

### 🔹 Conditionals (Lesson 25)
- `V-たら、～` (If / After doing something...).
- `V-て も、～` (Even if...).
